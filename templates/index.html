In a volatile stock market, low-cost financial advice and investment management can be more valuable than ever.
Robo-advisors can be an accessible way to get it:

for a fraction of what the typical financial advisor might charge, allowing you to be hands-off with your investments.

Get Started

What is a “Robo-Advisor”?

  • With the help of digital systems, a computerized questionnaire is filled out and the system creates the investment profile of the investor.
  • These systems clarify the risk levels and investment ranges that are appropriate for the client and purchase listed products for him.
  • These robotic systems are designed to be a replacement for the human service provided today, by portfolio managers and investment houses.
  • Portfolio management is a service that competes with investment consulting and therefore in the future digital portfolio management may also compete with private investment consultants.
